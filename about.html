About SmartGulak

SmartGulak is not just an expense tracker – it’s your personal financial companion. Designed especially for students and young professionals, it helps you track expenses, set budgets, and save smarter with a clean and simple interface.

Whether it’s managing your monthly pocket money or keeping an eye on daily spending, SmartGulak gives you the insights you need to stay in control of your finances.

Key Highlights

  • Expense Tracking – Record daily expenses in seconds.
  • Smart Budgeting – Plan monthly budgets and avoid overspending.
  • Insightful Reports – Visualize where your money goes.
  • Secure & Private – Your data stays with you.

This project is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as a demo frontend application, showcasing modern UI design and practical financial tools.
